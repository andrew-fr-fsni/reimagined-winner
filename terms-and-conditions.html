1. Introduction and who we are

The Foodies Foundation (we or the Foodies Foundation) is a Trust that has been established to help Foodies (including Foodies Co-operative and Foodies Store employees and their families) in need. The Foodies Foundation is governed and administered by Trustees acting in accordance with a Trust Deed.

These terms and conditions apply to:

  • the use of this Foodies Foundation website;
  • the collection and use of any personal information by the Foodies Foundation;
  • any donations you make to the Foodies Foundation; and
  • any application for funding made to the Foodies Foundation.

2. Privacy

Any personal data or personal information you transmit to this site by electronic mail or otherwise (or which the Foodies Foundation otherwise collects from you), will be used by the Foodies Foundation in accordance with our Privacy Policy in section 9 below.

3. Making a donation

You can make a donation to the Foodies Foundation via this website and through other means notified or accepted by the Foodies Foundation.

Receipts for online one-off donations are provided via email once the payment has been processed.

Regular or on-going donation receipts will be issued by the Foodies Foundation to on-going donors after the end of each financial year.

4. Refund and cancellation policy

All transactions are processed in $NZ dollars.

One-off donations cannot be refunded (unless otherwise determined by the Foodies Foundation Trustees).

If you wish to cancel a regular or on-going donation you must contact us. We will process cancellation of on-going donations as soon as we reasonably can.

If you have made a donation you propose to withdraw or dispute, please contact us at the contact details set out in section 11 below.

6. Applying for funding from the Foodies Foundation

You can apply for funding to the Foodies Foundation via this website and through other means notified or accepted by the Foodies Foundation. Funding and eligibility criteria will be as determined by the Foodies Foundation Trustees from time to time.

9. Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy section of these terms and conditions applies to all information collected by the Foodies Foundation, whether through this website or otherwise.

The Foodies Foundation takes privacy and data protection seriously. The Foodies Foundation recognises the importance of protecting an individual's privacy and is fully committed to following the Privacy Act 1993 and the Privacy Principles set out in that Act.

This Privacy Policy tells you what information we collect and why, what we do with it, and your rights to view, correct or change it.

What type of information does the Foodies Foundation collect?

Except for the ‘visitor information’ mentioned below, we do not collect personally identifying information about you when you visit our site, unless you choose to provide such information to us (e.g. through making a donation to the Foodies Foundation). Providing such information is strictly voluntary. This policy is your guide to how we will handle information we collect from you.

Visitor information

We collect and store the following information about visitors to our site: the name of the domain from which you access the Internet, the date and time you access our site, and the Internet address of the website from which you linked to our site. Cookies may be used to remember visitor preferences when interacting with the website.

We use the information we collect to measure the number of visitors to the different sections of our site, and to help us make our site more useful to visitors.

Information we collect when you make a donation

The Foodies Foundation collects and uses personal information from donors including: names, addresses, telephone numbers, email addresses and donation dates/amounts.

We use this information to:

  • provide and distribute receipts;
  • send out donor acknowledgement letters and thank donors for their donations;
  • keep supporters informed about upcoming fundraising initiatives and other activities of the Foodies Foundation; and
  • communicate to our supporters through direct mail and email.

Information we collect when you apply for funding from the Foodies Foundation

If you apply to the Foodies Foundation for funding, we will ask you for a range of personal information including name, addresses, telephone numbers, email addresses, your personal, family and financial circumstances, and why you are seeking funding.

We use this information to help the Foodies Foundation make appropriate decisions in relation to who we give funding to.

Sending us an email

You also may decide to send us personally identifying information, for example, in an email containing a question or comment, or by filling out a web form that provides us this information. We use this information from an email primarily to respond to your requests. We may forward your email to other employees who are better able to answer your questions.

If you sign up for one of our email lists, we will only send you the kinds of information you have requested. We won't share your name or email address with any outside parties.

Opt-out or change your contact information

If you are receiving communication from us that you wish to update, request a correction to your personal information or you no longer wish to receive then please contact us at the contact details set out at section 11 below.

You will always have the option to opt-in and opt-out of communications at any time.
